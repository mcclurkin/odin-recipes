Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish made with alternating layers of pasta, cheese, sauce, and meat. While it's usually served as a main dish, it can also be served as a side dish or appetizer. Lasagna is a great meal to make ahead of time and freeze for later use. It's also a great dish to make for a large group of people, as it can be made in large quantities and is easy to serve.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste

Steps

  1. Saute onions and garlic
  2. Add meat and brown
  3. Add tomatoes, tomato paste, 1/2 cup water
  4. Add salt, pepper, parsley, basil, oregano
  5. Cover and simmer 1 hour, 15 minutes
