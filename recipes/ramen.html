Ramen Recipe

Description

Ramen is a Japanese noodle soup. It consists of Chinese wheat noodles served in a meat or (occasionally) fish-based broth, often flavored with soy sauce or miso, and uses toppings such as sliced pork (叉焼 chāshū), nori (dried seaweed), menma, and scallions. Nearly every region in Japan has its own variation of ramen, from the tonkotsu (pork bone broth) ramen of Kyushu to the miso ramen of Hokkaido.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of ramen noodles
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup of chopped green onions
  • 1/2 cup of chopped mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup of chopped carrots
  • 1/2 cup of chopped bok choy
  • 1/2 cup of chopped tofu
  • 1/2 cup of chopped pork

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Add noodles and cook for 3 minutes
  3. Add vegetables and cook for 2 minutes
  4. Add egg and cook for 1 minute
  5. Add seasoning and serve
